Twice Albums

More & More

TWICE announces ninth mini-album and title song 'MORE & MORE' on June 1! It is a comeback nine months after the previous work'Feel Special' and the first new song released in 2020 will quench the thirst of music fans.

Feel Special

TWICE announces 8th mini album and title song 'Feel Special' of the same name! The title song'Feel Special' sang the thrilling moments of precious people when they walked through the tunnel of a dark heart like a black night.

Fancy You

TWICE announces the 7th mini album 'FANCY YOU' & title song 'FANCY'! The title song 'FANCY' is a mellow mood pop dance song that shows the musical change of TWICE with a concept of color that is distinctly different from what TWICE showed so far.

The year of Yes

TWICE releases its third special album'The year of "YES'" on December 12! The title track of the special 3rd album, 'The year of "YES",'The Best Thing of the Year' is a song that combines the alternative R&B genre and TWICE's pop sound with a strong groove.

Twicelights

TWICE World Tour 2019 'TWICELIGHTS' is the first world tour held by TWICE. The first show was held on May 25, 2019, at KSPO Dome in Seoul, South Korea.

Beyond Live - TWICE: World In A Day

TWICE will soon be greeting fans through an online concert! On July 17, TWICE’s official Twitter announced news of the group’s online concert “Beyond LIVE – TWICE : World in A Day.”

Introduction to TWICE

They were formed through the competition reality show SIXTEEN and officially debuted on October 20, 2015 with the mini album The Story Begins.
